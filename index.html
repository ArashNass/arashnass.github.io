About

I'm Arash, a Director at Willis (WTW), where I assess how buildings and infrastructure hold up against natural perils, mainly earthquake, flood, windstorm, tsunami, and hailstorm. I also consult on natural catastrophe and risk engineering assessment. Before this, I taught earthquake engineering at University College London (UCL).

My work has taken me from the fault lines of the Pacific Ring of Fire to the flood plains of Northern Europe and the storm belts of the Caribbean. These tools are a way to share that experience in an interactive, hands-on way.
